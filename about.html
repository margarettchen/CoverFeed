    Why CoverFeed?

    CoverFeed is different. We want to make your life easier. Event planning can be difficult and it's just as difficult to discover new events that matter to you. That's where we come in. We want to simplify collecting entrance fees for organizers and make it easier to discover meaningful events near you.
